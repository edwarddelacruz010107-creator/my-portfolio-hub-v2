Operating principles

Professional by default.

  • Clear ownership: creators control their portfolio content and public presentation.
  • Secure foundations: authentication, tenant boundaries, uploads, and billing workflows are treated as core product concerns.
  • Accessible publishing: responsive layouts, alt text, readable typography, and reduced-motion support are built into the experience.
  • Transparent policies: public privacy and terms pages explain how accounts, content, and service availability are handled.
