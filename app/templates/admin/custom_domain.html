DNS setup guide

After saving a domain, add both DNS records at your domain provider. TXT proves ownership; CNAME routes traffic to MyPortfolioHub.

  • Wait a few minutes after saving DNS records before verifying.
  • One verified domain can only belong to one tenant.
  • Your host must accept the custom domain and SSL must be configured in production.
