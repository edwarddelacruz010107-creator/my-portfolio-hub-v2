{# ── CONTACT FORM PROVIDER (Basin) ────────────────────────────────────── #}

Contact Form Provider — Basin

Basin (usebasin.com) handles portfolio contact form submissions per tenant. Each tenant configures their own Basin endpoint in their admin settings.
The default is Internal CMS — submissions are stored in the database.

Applied to new tenants. Existing tenants use their own setting.
