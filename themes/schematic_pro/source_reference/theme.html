SPEC.01 — ABOUT

I like systems that don't fall over.

Most of my work lives in the unglamorous middle layer of a product — the part between "the design looks great" and "the feature actually works in production." I spend a lot of time on authentication flows, billing logic, and the migration scripts nobody wants to write, because that's usually where a SaaS app quietly breaks.

On the design side, I care about interfaces that stay legible under real data — long names, empty states, five tenants instead of one. If a component only looks good with placeholder text, it isn't finished.
