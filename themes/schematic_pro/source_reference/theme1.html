About Me

Engineering reliable software, end to end.

I'm a full-stack developer specializing in Flask-based SaaS platforms and point-of-sale systems. My work spans the entire stack — from PostgreSQL schema design and multi-tenant architecture to secure payment integrations and polished frontend interfaces.

I approach every project as a system: identifying constraints, mapping components, and building with maintainability and security as first-class requirements — not afterthoughts. I favor clean architecture, thorough audits, and production-grade standards over quick hacks.

When I'm not shipping code, you'll find me on two wheels — competitive road and criterium cycling keeps the same discipline of pacing, strategy, and endurance alive outside the terminal.
